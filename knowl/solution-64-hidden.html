1. \(\displaystyle 5^\boxed{2} = 25 \quad\Rightarrow\quad \log_{5} (25) = \boxed{2}\)
  2. \(\displaystyle 5^{\boxed{-1}} = \frac{1}{5} \quad\Rightarrow\quad \log_{5} \left(\frac{1}{5}\right) = \boxed{-1}\)
  3. \(\displaystyle \left(\frac{1}{5}\right)^{\boxed{-1}} = 5 \quad\Rightarrow\quad \log_{\frac{1}{5}} \left(5\right) = \boxed{-1}\)
  4. \(\displaystyle 5^{\boxed{1/2}} = \sqrt{5} \quad\Rightarrow\quad \log_{5} \left(\sqrt{5}\right) = \boxed{1/2}\)
  5. \(\displaystyle 5^{\boxed{1}} = 5 \quad\Rightarrow\quad \log_{5} \left(5\right) = \boxed{1}\)
  6. \(\displaystyle 5^{\boxed{0}} = 1 \quad\Rightarrow\quad \log_{5} \left(1\right) = \boxed{0}\)
  7. \(5^x \gt 0\text{,}\) for all \(x\text{.}\) In particular, \(5^x \neq -1\) for any \(x\text{.}\) Thus \(\log_{5} \left(-1\right)\) is undefined.
