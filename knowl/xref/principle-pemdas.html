Principle 1.9. Order of Operations.

Work from left to right evaluating the expression respecting the following order:
  1. Parentheses: Perform all calculations inside parentheses or brackets (if parentheses are nested, work from inside out);
  2. Exponents: Evaluate all exponential expressions \(a^b\text{;}\)
  3. Multiplication and Division: Evaluate all products and quotients;
  4. Addition and Subtraction: Evaluate all sums and differences.
