Warning 2.2. Division by Zero is Undefined.

You must never divide a real number by zero. Expressions such as “\(1/0\)” or “\(0/0\)” are considered to be undefined in that they are assigned no numeric value and have no mathematical meaning.
Division into zero by a non-zero real number is permitted and results in zero. For instance, \(0/1 = 0\text{.}\)
